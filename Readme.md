## Key Insights

- Traders showed *higher leverage and volume during greed phases*, but PnL often fell.  
- *Fear phases were marked by lower risk-taking* but steadier results.  
- *Extreme greed days are often followed by net negative PnL*, making them a potential contrarian signal.
